Home page

[SUMMARY OF WEBSITE (HOME) Welcome future and potential employers to Indys Portfolio! Thank you for coming here! Im so excited to have you here! To give you a quick summary of what this is: This is for you. I made this to show you what I am capable of in terms of graphic and web design. Please feel free to look at what I’ve accomplished. On the About Me page you’ll find all that you need to know about me, from where I grew up, graduated high school and college to what I’m currently accomplishing. On the Gallery page you’ll find all my most proud work I’ve done over the years. Feel free to look at everything there, I know you will appreciate it as much as I do. Thank you for taking the time to view this site! ]

[ABOUT ME Let me tell you about me! I am a native of Arizona, I grew up in Mesa. Im 26 years old. I attended Westwood High School 2011-2015 graduating from High School and a Trade School called E.V.I.T(East Valley Institute of Technology) graduating with a certificate of completion in graphic design. In 2018 I attended Scottsdale Community College and graduated with my associate degree in graphic design in December of 2022. I am currently attending BYU-I and getting my bachelor’s in software development. Learning software development has helped me see how very similar it is to graphic design. I’m applying a lot of what I have already done into all the new things I get to learn. Seeing and learning It has helped me tremendously in life, work and much more. It has made me a more confident person and I’m more willing to try new things, think outside the box, and give my all to it. ]
